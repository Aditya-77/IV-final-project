Visualization Goals

The Goal of the Visualization is to effectively communicate insights and trends related to the performance and characteristics of different mutual funds. To provide investors and other stakeholders with a clear and compelling picture of the returns provided by different funds, including the funds name, size, and performance over time and help viewers make more informed decisions about investing in mutual funds and better understand the risks and rewards associated with investing in mutual funds.

Intended Audience

Investor, people who are actively involved in investing and also people who are new to investing. Also financial analysts, journalists, etc.

Data Source

The data for this visualization has been taken from Tickertape.
